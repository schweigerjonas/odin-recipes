Oatmeal

Description

A delicious, highly personalizable Oatmeal providing a large amount of protein for optimal muscle growth.

Ingredients

Main

  • Fine Oats - 100g
  • Water - 300ml
  • Whey Protein Powder - 30g
  • Psyllium Husks - 5g
  • Salt
  • Cinnamon

Topping (Examples)

  • Blueberries
  • Raspberries
  • Dark Chocolate
  • Peanutbutter

Steps

  1. Put oats and water into a bowl and stir them
  2. Heat the bowl in your microwave for 2.5 minutes
  3. Add a pinch of salt, your protein powder and the psyllium husks and stir thoroughly
  4. Add cinnamon and all of your favourite toppings on top
